A stunt academy is a training facility or program where individuals learn the skills and techniques required to perform stunts in various forms of entertainment, such as movies, television, theater, and live performances. Stunt performers are highly skilled individuals who execute dangerous and physically demanding actions, often on behalf of actors or characters in the production.

It's important to note that becoming a professional stunt performer is a demanding career that requires dedication, physical fitness, and a commitment to safety. Many stunt performers start by attending a stunt academy to learn the basics before gaining practical experience on sets through internships, apprenticeships, or smaller productions.
